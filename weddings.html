Your Wedding Ceremony: Accompanied by Solo Violinist

Philip Heyman: Solo Wedding Violinist

Looking for the perfect musical accompaniment for your wedding? As a solo violinist, I offer enchanting performances that create an unforgettable atmosphere on your special day. With a repertoire that includes classic romantic pieces and contemporary favorites, I bring a touch of elegance to every ceremony and reception.

I perform with professional backing tracks that complement my live playing, ensuring a rich and immersive experience for you and your guests. Additionally, you have the option to enhance your sound with a live pianist, creating a beautiful blend of melodies that will resonate throughout your celebration.

Whether you envision a serene ambiance during the ceremony or lively tunes for the reception, I am dedicated to tailoring the music to your unique vision. Let me help make your wedding day magical with the timeless beauty of the violin.
