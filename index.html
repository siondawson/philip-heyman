Philip Heyman

Violinist and Violist in Cardiff

Experienced Orchestral Violist, Versatile Event Musician and Teacher.

I made my concerto debut at the age of eleven and pursued my studies at the Royal College of Music, where I was honored with three major viola prizes upon graduation.

My career began with a permanent position at the BBC Symphony Orchestra, with whom I toured extensively and performed in numerous Prom concerts. Subsequently, I served as Sub-Principal Viola with the English National Opera for nine years, collaborating with leading British orchestras and ensembles while also teaching and giving recitals.

From 1998 until 2021, I held the position of Principal Viola at Welsh National Opera, where I performed major orchestral solos on radio and television and served as a guest Principal with esteemed national orchestras like CBSO, RPO, BSO, RLPO, and BBCPO.

I continue to give solo recitals and offer consultations and private teaching, both independently and at Cardiff University. I am proud to be a member of the Cardiff-based viola quartet Absolute Zero and explore the repertoire for guitar, flute, and viola as a member of Still Life with Guitar.

In 2002, I acquired Europe's first Pellegrina pomposa model viola, a groundbreaking instrument that I am privileged to champion as a significant innovation in string design.

Absolute Zero Viola Quartet

Absolute Zero Viola Quartet stands as a pioneering force in contemporary chamber music, reshaping the classical landscape with their distinctive instrumentation and inventive interpretations. Established in 2011, this vibrant ensemble comprises four exceptional violists devoted to highlighting the versatility and depth of their instrument. Through a dedication to commissioning original compositions, arranging existing pieces, and collaborating with composers, Absolute Zero Viola Quartet captivates audiences with their emotive performances and forward-looking ethos. With adventurous programming and virtuosic artistry, they continually push the boundaries of the viola quartet genre, garnering accolades and admiration on a global scale.

Freelance Weddings & Functions

I am available on a freelance basis to play with string quartets, offering my skills as a versatile string player. My primary instrument is the viola, but I am also proficient in playing the violin. I have worked extensively with groups such as the Aderyn String Quartet and Kings Strings. If you need a reliable and experienced musician for your next performance, feel free to reach out.
